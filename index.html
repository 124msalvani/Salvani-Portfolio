My Inspirations

I was inspired by indie developers and people on Youtube who documented the process of making their own video games. I loved seeing them work out bugs and problems, create stunning environments and craft compelling and realistic characters. They always seemed so passionate about their games, and how they could tell their story. Indie developers succeeding in making their games encouraged me to learn how to code, so that I could someday express my imaginative stories through the medium of video games.
